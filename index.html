Bullying

Have you heard about bullying going around in your school? Bullying is a way to harm someone vulnerable. There are three different types of bullying. They are cyberbullying, verbal bullying, and physical bullying. On the other hand, there are many ways to prevent bullying. You can stsnd up to the bully, get an adult or a teacher to the scene, or you could walk away from the person bullying you.
