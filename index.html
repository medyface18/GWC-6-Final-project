Bullying


Have you heard about bullying going around in your school? Bullying is a way to harm someone vulnerable. There are three different types of bullying. They are cyberbullying, verbal bullying, and physical bullying. On the other hand, there are many ways to prevent bullying. You can stsnd up to the bully, get an adult or a teacher to the scene, or you could walk away from the person bullying you.



Cyberbullying


Cyberbullying is bullying online. When you send mean and hurtful text messegaes, it is a form of cyberbullying.


Verbal


Just like cyberbullying, verbal bullying is another form of bullying. Verbal bullying is bullying with words. When people spread rumors about another person, it is a form of verbal bullying. Another example is when people call other people names and saying mean things to them.



Physical


The third is physical bullying. This form of bullying is with physical force. An example would be punching or hurting someone and forcing them to do something they don't want to do.
